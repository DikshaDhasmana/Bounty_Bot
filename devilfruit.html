Devil Fruits

Devil Fruits are the special elements which either help in the battles and game play or help you in buying assets in exchange. The non purchasable devil fruits, on the other hand, are acquired through special tasks and quests.

1. Purchasable Devil Fruits: These are tradable fruits, and can either be purchased from shop for original price, or by drops, which may or may not grant a fruit for lower price. There are three attributes associated with the purchasable devil fruits, opponent damage multiplier, health booster and lucky charm. The higher the price, the more powerful the devil fruit.

2. Non-purchasable Devil Fruits: These are non tradable fruits which can only be acquired by completing quests and special tasks. Different assets can be purchased in exchange of collections of different such devil fruits. The more valuable the assets, the more the bounty of user.

There are three types of Purchasable Devil Fruits: Paramecia, Mythical Zoan and Logia. Click on the links below to view more info.

Paramecia Fruits

Fruits of this type allows the consumer to achieve one of a large variety of abilities that enables them to become "superhuman" or perform a superhuman feat.

Mythical Zoans

This type of Devil Fruit allows a user to transform into an animal species at will, or an inter-species hybrid.

Logia Fruits

Those who have eaten a Logia fruit are granted the power to transform their body's composition into a natural element at will, as well as create and control it.
